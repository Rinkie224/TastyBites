Proposal 
1. Overview  
NAME: Tasty Bites 
Brief History: 
Tasty Bites is a delicious hot dog restaurant established in 2026. It focuses on providing quick, 
affordable delicious meals for customers. The business started as a small takeaway outlet 
and now has become popular among students and working individuals due to quality food and 
best service. What started as a passion project at a local weekend market has grown into a 
beloved hotdog destination with two thriving locations. 
The founder believed that fast food doesn't have to mean low quality. Every Tasty Bites hotdog is 
handcrafted with care, using locally sourced ingredients, and served with genuine warmth. 
Mission: 
To provide high-quality, affordable street food that brings the community together. To serve the most 
flavourful, high-quality hotdogs using sustainably sourced ingredients while creating a welcoming space 
for families, friends, and food lovers. 
Vision: 
To become the most recognized hot dog brand in the region known for flavour and quality. Also be 
South Africa's leading gourmet hotdog brand, recognised for exceptional taste, community engagement, 
and sustainable practices. 
Target Audience: -Students -Working professionals -Families -People looking for quick and affordable meals 
2. Website Goals and Objectives 
The main goal of the Tasty Bites website is to create an online presence and make it easier for 
customers to access menu information and place enquiries. 
Specific Goals: -Display menu items online -Attract more clients -Provide contact and location information -Allow online enquiries or orders - Provide essential location and contact information to drive foot traffic. 
Key Performance Indicators (KPIs): -Website traffic -Customer enquiries -Menu page views -Increase in store visits - Number of menu downloads or clicks on the "Menu" page. 
3. Current Website Analysis 
Tasty Bites currently does not have sponsorship on big websites and relies on small liable customers and 
social media posts. 
Strengths: -Strong local customer base -Affordable pricing -Good reputation 
Weaknesses: -Small reliable online presence -Limited accessibility of menu 
Areas for Improvement: 
Creating a professional website -Improve an online menu -Improve customer communication and concerns 
4. Proposed Website Features and Functionality 
Essential Features: 
Homepage (index.html): Welcome message, featuring a Hero section with a high-resolution image of the 
signature Tasty Bite hotdog, a brief brand introduction, and a primary call-to-action (CTA) button leading 
to the menu. 
About Us (about.html): Detailed history of the brand, our commitment to local ingredients, and an 
introduction to the founding team. 
Menu Page(menu.html): A visually rich catalog of our hotdog varieties, including descriptions of unique 
toppings and side dishes. 
Enquiry Page (enquiry.html): A professional form for users to request catering services or private event 
bookings. 
Contact Page (contact.html): Includes a Google Maps integration showing our primary locations, contact 
phone numbers, and a general message form  
Additional Features: -Mobile-friendly design -Simple navigation -Call-to-action buttons (Order Now) 
5. Design and User Experience 
Design Aesthetic: 
Bright, attractive, and visually appealing with food imagery. A vibrant street-food theme using high
contrast elements and bold imagery to evoke an appetizing and modern atmosphere. 
Color Scheme: 
Red, yellow, and white - with Charcoal Grey for text readability. 
Typography: 
Bold and readable fonts - Bold sans-serif headings (Montserrat) for impact and clean, highly readable body text (Aptos) for 
descriptions. 
Layout: -Clean layout with large food images 
-Easy navigation 
User Experience Considerations: -Fast loading pages -Easy access to menu -Clear contact details 
User Experience: Priority is placed on mobile-first design, ensuring the menu is easily readable on 
smartphones. A fixed navigation bar will allow users to jump between pages instantly. 
Low-Fidelity Wireframe Descriptions 
Homepage Wireframe: 
Header: Logo on the left, horizontal navigation links on the right. 
Hero Section: A full-width placeholder for a high-quality image with centered text and a "View Our 
Menu" button. 
Introduction: A three-column layout briefly highlighting "Fresh Ingredients," "Unique Flavors," and 
"Local Roots." 
Footer: Social media icons and copyright information. 
Products Page Wireframe: 
Grid Layout: A 3x2 grid of product cards. Each card contains a square image placeholder, a bold title, a 
short description, and a price tag. 
Enquiry Page Wireframe: 
Form Layout: A single-column vertical form with input fields for Name, Email, Event Date, and a text 
area for "Special Requirements."
